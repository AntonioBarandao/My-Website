Project Overview

Our project focuses on creating a campus events webpage where users can submit event details such as the title, date, location, and description. Submitted events are displayed on the webpage, allowing students and staff to easily stay informed about upcoming activities, meetings, and workshops. The goal is to improve communication and increase event participation across campus.
